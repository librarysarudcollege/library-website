About the Library

Established in 1983 concurrently with the college, the Library of Shri Shiv-Shahu Mahavidyalaya, Sarud, serves as the central hub for academic resources and scholarly activity. Our mission is to provide comprehensive services and resources essential to meeting the diverse academic and research needs of our users.

📚 Collection and Resources

The library maintains an excellent, multifaceted collection encompassing the Faculties of Arts, Commerce, and Science, supplemented by a robust offering of journals and non-book reading materials. In line with modern educational demands, our service philosophy emphasizes a qualitative approach to user support.

💻 Technology and Accessibility

The library leverages modern infrastructure to enhance access and efficiency. Bar Code Technology is implemented to streamline book transactions. The dedicated Digital Library component, equipped with two internet-connected computers, provides a platform for accessing global knowledge. Furthermore, through our subscription to INFLIBNET NLIST, faculty, students, and research scholars gain access to a rich pool of professional e-journals and e-books.

🏛️ Facilities and Membership

Our Reading Hall offers a tranquil and encouraging ambiance, comfortably accommodating over 40 students for focused study. Membership is extended not only to enrolled students, researchers, and existing faculties but also to outsiders committed to scholarly pursuit. The library operates from 8:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. throughout the year, remaining closed only on Sundays and public holidays.

🎯 OUR MISSION

"To provide the right information to the right user at the right time in the right format, enabling success in career-oriented courses through the acquisition, organization, and dissemination of knowledge resources."
